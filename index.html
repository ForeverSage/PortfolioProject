About Me
My name is Tyler Gajdocek. I am a 32y.o. machine programmer from White Bear Lake, MN. I am a longtime technology enthusiast with an appetite for knowledge who is on a journey to understand how things work.

I love skateboarding, cryptocurrency and gaming as well as spending time with my wife and daughter.
I am the devoted father of a beautiful 9 month old baby girl named Gemma. She was born a few weeks early on July 20th, 2022! She is very energetic happy little baby that gets into everything. Gotta keep an eye on her all the time or your glasses or the TV's power cable will become her new favorite toy.
I am the son of two amazing and hardworking parents. I'm lucky to able to get together with them at least once every week. They've owned several businesses and taught me that if you're willing to put in the work, you can do just about anything.

To make up for being a less than stellar son for many years of my life, I help them out whenever possible.
I am the loving husband of my wife, Lauren. We dated briefly back in 2010 and met up again over 9 years later. We were married at the start of the pandemic at her parent's house where she showed up, on our wedding day, without a dress since all the wedding dress stores were closed.

She made my original (temporary) wedding ring out of a skateboard she had bought me back in 2010. A year later she surprised me with a custom ring with a bit more lasting power.
